The assert is used to test.

assertEqual  -> assertEqual(5,5)
assertTrue   -> assertTrue([1,2,3])
assertFalse  -> assertFalse([1,2,3])
assertIs     -> assertIs(a,b) (__eq__)
assertIsNone -> assertIsNone(None)
assertIn(a,b) -> assertIn('string','This is a string')
assertIsInstance -> assertIsInstance(c,d)
